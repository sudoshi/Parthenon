# Patient Timelines

The **Patient Timelines** feature provides an interactive, event-level view of an individual patient's clinical history drawn from the OMOP CDM. It displays all clinical events — conditions, drugs, procedures, measurements, visits, observations — on a chronological timeline, enabling clinical review, phenotype validation, and case study work.

## Access Requirements

Patient Timelines displays **Protected Health Information (PHI)** and requires:

- The **Researcher** role (minimum) or higher
- A data source with a properly configured CDM daimon
- Explicit administrator enablement of patient-level access for your account

If you do not see the **Patients** menu item, contact your administrator to request access.

## Finding a Patient

Navigate to **Patients → Patient Timelines**. You can locate patients by:

- **Person ID** — direct lookup by OMOP `person_id` (integer)
- **Cohort membership** — browse patients within a generated cohort

When browsing cohort patients, the list shows person IDs sorted by cohort start date. Click any patient to open their timeline.

:::warning PHI handling
All patient searches and timeline views are logged in the audit log with your user ID and timestamp. Do not share patient-identifiable information outside your authorized data use agreement.
:::

## Timeline View

The timeline shows events on a horizontal time axis with:

- **Color-coded event bands** — each domain has a distinct color (conditions = purple, drugs = orange, measurements = teal, visits = blue, procedures = green)
- **Observation periods** — shown as gray bars behind events indicating periods of continuous observation
- **Zoom controls** — click-drag to zoom into a specific time window; double-click to zoom out
- **Event tooltips** — hover over any event to see concept ID, concept name, source value, and dates

## Event Detail Panel

Click any event to open the detail panel, which shows all available CDM fields for that record:

- Concept IDs and names (standard and source)
- Dates (start, end, visit context)
- Type concept, quantity, dose, units (domain-specific fields)
- Visit link — click to highlight all events from the same visit

## Filtering the Timeline

Use the **Filter** panel (right sidebar) to show/hide domains, date ranges, or specific concept sets. For example, to focus on diabetes-related events:

1. Click **Filter by Concept Set**
2. Select your "Type 2 Diabetes" concept set
3. The timeline highlights matching events and fades unrelated events

## Export

Click **Export Timeline** to download the patient's event list as a CSV file for offline review or case narrative writing.
