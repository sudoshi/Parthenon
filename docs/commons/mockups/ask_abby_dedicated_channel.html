Welcome to the team! Here's a summary of the CKD Progression study:

Status: Active — currently in multi-site execution across 4 of 6 enrolled sites. Sites A, B, D, and E are running; Site C is pending IRB amendment approval, Site F completed data quality checks last week.

Key design decisions:
• The team chose a 90-day washout for ACE inhibitor exposure based on dispensing data precision (Dr. Patel led this, Nov 3 discussion in #ckd-progression)
• eGFR slope was selected as the primary outcome over time-to-ESKD after a review in December — the rationale was statistical power with the available follow-up period
• Patients with AKI events in the 30 days pre-index are excluded (added after Dr. Torres flagged confounding in the initial results)
